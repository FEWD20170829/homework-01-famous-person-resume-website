"A party without cake is really just a meeting."

In the kitchen of La Pitchoune, my home in the South of France.


Bonjour! I'm Julia Child, an American-born chef and author of the bestselling cookbook Mastering the Art of French Cooking. You may also know me from my long-running TV show, The French Chef, or from Meryl Streep's portrayal of me in the 2009 film Julie & Julia, released five years after my death.

I fell in love with French food in the late 1940s, learned how to cook it, and never turned back.
